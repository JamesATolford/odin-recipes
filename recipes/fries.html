Home Fries

Recipe

prep: 5mins

cook: 20mins

total: 25mins

servings: 4

yield: 4 serving

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 0 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  1. step 1: Rinse potato cubes with cold water and drain well.
  2. step 2: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Place potatoes in the skillet and season with salt and pepper; mix well. Cover and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. step 3: Remove the lid and cook, turning frequently, until brown and crisp on all sides, about 10 more minutes.
